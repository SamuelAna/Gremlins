Gremlins (1984)

Gremlins is the story of a man who buys his son, Billy, a new pet. It’s an innocent looking creature, called a mogwai.

Billy immediatly takes to the teddy bear like creature with it's cute singing and names it Gizmo. Gizmo however, comes with 3 rules:

  1. Avoid btight lights, especially sunlight, which can kill it.
  2. Don’t get it wet
  3. No feeding after midnight.

Billy breaks rules 2 and 3, which cause the mogwai to multiply, and change into an evil hideous creature known as a gremlin, respectively.

From there the film takes a dark turn, as the Gremlins descend on the town attacking people, destroying things, and generally wreaking havoc.
